Switching to the Messaging tutorial. First, discard any unsaved changes from the previous tutorial. The Bird and Nest tutorial demonstrates how widgets communicate. Watch the Show Me animation. A number is dragged onto a bird. The bird flies to its matching nest. The nest collects each message in a queue. Click the magnifier on the nest to see what has arrived. Practice sending multiple messages and inspecting the nest contents. After covering these basics, we can now move on to programming by example, by training robots.
