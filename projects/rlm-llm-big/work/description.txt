# Building Family Trees from War and Peace with AI

**Video URL:** [TBD]
**Duration:** 6:36

---

War and Peace is far too large for any LLM context window. This video demonstrates RLM Level 4 processing a 3MB novel to extract noble family relationships. The orchestrating LLM delegates analysis to sub-LLMs, reducing millions of characters to structured family trees. Two demos show the process: a command line walkthrough and a web visualizer with real-time progress tracking. Watch as RLM identifies the Rostov, Bolkonsky, and other noble families through recursive analysis.
