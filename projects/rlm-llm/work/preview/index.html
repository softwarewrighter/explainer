The Setup: Lord Edward Ashford III found dead in his study.

The Suspects: Seven people were in the house that night. Each has a story. Each has something to hide.

The Question: Can a Language Model analyze the witness statements and identify the killer.

No spoilers. Watch the demonstration or run it yourself.

Video Concept

Level 4: LLM Delegation

What Level 4 does: Delegates reasoning tasks to other Language Models

This demo is NOT about: Chunking large data, context window limits, megabytes of text

Perfect for: Problems needing judgment, analysis, comparison, deduction

Demo focus: Murder mystery that requires reasoning across multiple witness statements
