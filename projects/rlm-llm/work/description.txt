A murder at the manor. Seven suspects. Dozens of clues. One killer. This video demonstrates RLM Level 4, where the orchestrating LLM delegates reasoning to sub-LLMs. Instead of code execution, the model calls other models to analyze witness statements, compare alibis, and draw conclusions. Watch as L4 examines each suspect and identifies the killer.
